Features and Services

Geospatial Visualisation

Geospatial AQI data is collected through the day and displayed on a choropleth map in addition to a marker that displays the current location of the sensing modules.

Weekly Trends

An interactive chart that displays results of the last seven days to simplify and highlight the changes in air quality throught the week.

Live Updates of Pollutant Concentrations

The concentrations of specific gaases in the atmosphere are periodically sensed, analysed, and presented in a dynamic manner.

Tips and Advice

AtmoSense provides general recommendations in addition to advice specific to the people most at risk based on the current AQI.



About AtmoSense

Bahrain's First Air Monitoring System

Most people have health issues but are unable to determine the cause of their symptoms. According to research, constant exposure to poor air quality is strongly associated with heart and lung problems such as shortness of breath and even change in blood chemistry leading to clots and heart attacks. The first step of solving this problem is continous monitoring to determine which areas have the worst air quality, guiding pepole to best practices, then informing policy makers so preventative measures can be taken. Unfortunately, there isn't an air quality monitoring system that covers the entire nation due to the exorbitant expense of conventional systems. The purpose of AtmoSense is to provide a solution that measures the air quality around Bahrain and displays up-to-date information in a user friendly manner.
